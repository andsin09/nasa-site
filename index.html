Basic Information

Apollo 20 is a 6-man team of students at Morris County Vocational School of Technology in the Computer Science Academy. We are focused on doing our best to get a good product out for the NASA App Development Challenge. Our goals are to do the best we can - make the best application, best site, and do the best we can to spread awarness of the Artemis 2 mission and our project. While winning is a tough condition to achieve, we know it is possible and thus are motivated to win. We try our best with every change we make to reach the goal of winning.

Site Info

So now, you're on this site. Why, you may ask? A simple answer. This site was created with the goal of informing the general public about the NASA ADC - it is a somewhat convoluted, unknown challenge to most, so this site is here to explain away to anyone who wants to know what it is about. This site was also made with the intention of helping anyone interested find out who our team is - we are on team of many out there, so we obviously are mostly unknown. We are Apollo 20, a six man team of students. We are Apollo 20, a team dedicated to winning. Welcome to the site.
